SARAH K ..Singer's family

Sarah K is a Kenyan born award-winning gospel music Minister, Vocalist, songwriter and an international Worship Leader, Mother of three and mother of many spiritual sons/daughters. She is a Pastor's wife with Anglican church of kenya.

Sarah K's Children

Sarah K is a celebrated and award-winning gospel music minister in Kenya. In a recent social media post, she could not hide her joy as a mother after her daughter got married. Sarah K also revealed that it was not easy to see her only daughter leave and in addition stating that it is biblical and the joy of every parent.

This a detailed information of our gospel singer Sarah K and her family of three children.She is a legend in the Kenyan gospel industry. Even with the changing industry dynamics and the new crop of artists who have taken the limelight, she has maintained her standards.

Meet the Singer Sarah K

Award-winning gospel singer Sarah K, whose real name is Sarah Kiarie, has continued to dazzle many by her amazingly talented voice and her reputation as a prolific song writer. There's no doubt that she is a force to reckon in the Gospel music industry following her breakout hit song Liseme and her countless praise and worship songs. Away from the lime-light, the mellow-voiced singer is also a loving mother to three adorable children a fact which not many know. Once in a while Sarah Kiarie takes to social media to appreciate her daughter and three sons, who prove truly the apple doesn't fall quite far from the tree.
